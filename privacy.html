Plain-language summary: EduPath collects your details only to process your university application in Malaysia. We never sell your data. You can request deletion at any time.

1. Who We Are

EduPath is an independent education consultancy that helps international students apply to universities and language institutes in Malaysia. We operate as an intermediary between students and accredited Malaysian institutions.

References to "EduPath," "we," "us," or "our" in this policy mean the EduPath business and its operators.

2. Data We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Identity Full name, date of birth, nationality, gender
  • Contact Email address, phone / WhatsApp number
  • Travel Passport number, passport expiry date
  • Academic Highest education level, field of study, English proficiency test scores (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Financial Bank statement (submitted only when required by a specific university)
  • Documents Scanned passport copy, academic transcripts, personal photo, any additional documents you upload

Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser type and language preference
  • Pages visited and time on site (anonymised analytics only)
  • Device type (mobile / desktop)

We do not collect payment card numbers, passwords of third-party services, or any biometric data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To process and submit your university or language institute application
  • To communicate with you about your application status, required documents, and next steps
  • To coordinate with Malaysian institutions on your behalf
  • To assist with EMGS Student Pass (visa) processing where applicable
  • To arrange accommodation or airport pickup if requested
  • To send service-related updates via WhatsApp or email (no unsolicited marketing)

We process your data on the basis of contract performance (to deliver the service you requested) and consent (for optional communications). You may withdraw consent at any time.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We share your personal data only when necessary and only with the following parties:

  • Malaysian universities & language institutes — to process your admission application
  • EMGS (Education Malaysia Global Services) — for Student Pass applications as required by law
  • Accommodation providers — if you request housing assistance
  • Airport pickup partners — if you request transportation on arrival
  • Supabase (cloud database provider) — to securely store application data (servers located in the EU/US; Supabase is GDPR-compliant)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for commercial purposes.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a secured cloud database (Supabase) using industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit (TLS). Access is restricted to authorised EduPath staff only.

We retain your data for as long as your application is active and for up to 3 years afterward for legal and audit purposes, unless you request earlier deletion.

While we implement strong security measures, no online system is 100% infallible. We will notify you promptly in the event of any data breach affecting your information.

6. Your Rights

Under the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) and applicable international standards, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request erasure of your data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent for processing at any time
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the details in Section 10. We will respond within 14 business days.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to remember your language preference and session state. No tracking cookies or advertising pixels are used.

  • lang — your language preference (AR/EN/JA/KO/RU)
  • ep_student_session — your student portal login session (cleared on logout)

You can clear local storage at any time via your browser settings without affecting your application on our servers.

8. Minors

Our services are intended for students aged 17 and above. If you are under 18, a parent or guardian must provide consent before submitting an application. We will delete any data submitted by a minor without verifiable parental consent upon discovery.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. For significant changes, we will notify active applicants directly.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, data requests, or complaints:

  • WhatsApp: Contact us via the WhatsApp button on our website
  • Website: www.edupath.my

We take all privacy concerns seriously and will respond as quickly as possible.
