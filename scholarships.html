Pro Tips

💡 How to Maximise Your Scholarship Chances

📄

Start Your SOP Early

A compelling Statement of Purpose is the most important document. Start writing 3 months before the deadline and get multiple revisions from advisors.

📊

Maintain a Strong CGPA

Most scholarships require a minimum CGPA of 3.2–3.5. Aim for 3.7+ to be competitive for the top government and corporate scholarships.

🏆

Build Your Portfolio

Research publications, community service, leadership roles, and relevant internships significantly strengthen your scholarship application.

📅

Apply to Multiple Scholarships

Don't put all your eggs in one basket. Apply to 5–10 scholarships simultaneously to maximise your chances of success.
